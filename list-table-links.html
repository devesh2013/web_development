Nature’s Seasonal Cycle

serial season description
1. summer the warmest season of the year, in the northern hemisphere from June to August and in the southern hemisphere from December to February. "this plant flowers in late summer"
2. winter Winter is the coldest season of the year, occurring between autumn and spring, defined by shorter days and lower temperatures caused by the Earth’s axis tilting away from the sun
3. autumn Autumn (or fall) is the transition season between summer and winter, characterized by cooling temperatures, decreasing daylight, and falling leaves as trees prepare for winter.
4. spring Spring is the temperate season of renewal and transition between winter and summer, characterized by warming temperatures, blooming plants, and lengthening daylight
